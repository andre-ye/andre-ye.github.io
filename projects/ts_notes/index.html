Transition School Notes

Repository of notes from my TS year.

The Transition School is a one-year intensive program at the Robinson Center which prepares students coming out of eighth grade for freshman year at the University of Washington. I’ve been pushing my notes for each of the courses to this site. I hope it will be of use to current TS students even if the curriculum has changed, and of interest to prospective TS students.
