## To the Incoming TS Student

I'm writing this post-TS letter - part self reflection, part utilitarian advice - on June 10th, 2021, one day after the last formal academic gathering of TS. Today, at six-thirty, the principal will host an end-of-year celebration to conclude three quarters of learning and growth. After then, TS will be *over* - and it's a bizarre feeling to experience. All things must come to an end, but of all things TS seems like it warps our conceptions of time in the most intruiging ways. I have grown so much and so quickly in this span of a few months, and the vacuum at its conclusion, the silence after the year-long storm, leaves me wanting and my sense of time distorted. I see so much ahead, and the forceful hand of time pushes me to encounter my vision of the future.
