## To the Incoming TS Student

I'm writing this post-TS letter - part self reflection, part utilitarian advice - on June 10th, 2021, one day after the last formal academic gathering of TS. Today, at six-thirty, the principal will host an end-of-year celebration to conclude three quarters of learning and growth. After then, TS will be *over* - and it's a bizarre feeling to experience. All things must come to an end, but of all things TS seems like it warps our conceptions of time in the most intruiging ways. The silence after the boom
